Context

Aliens have surrounded you and your team. Your only salvation was to hide inside an abbandoned bunker. After exploring in the dark, you reach inside a big, square room, lit by several red emergency lights. The room is filled with old racks and abbandoned workstations. At the other side of the room you can see a monitor, glooming with a green font. The corpse of a female doctor lies on the floor. In her hand a wrinkled piece of paper reports: Only hope for humanity. Recover the system and execute the purge procedure. User password: recovery, recovery

Note

You should disable Vimperator or similar plugins or enter in insert mode. Initial credential are recovery:recovery (as stated in the context).
